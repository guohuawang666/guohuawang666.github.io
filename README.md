# About Me

I am Guohua Wang, a PhD candidate at the School of Architecture, Southwest Jiaotong University, China. My research focuses on rail transit, climate and thermal comfort, subway hub blocks, and urban microclimate.

## Research Interests
- Rail transit and urban spatial interaction
- Climate and thermal comfort in urban environments
- Spatial design and environmental performance of subway hub blocks
- Transit-oriented development (TOD)
- Urban microclimate and pedestrian comfort
